Every conversation starts from zero. It doesn't have to.

01

This is what your AI actually knows about you. Not tucked away in some hidden vector store — right here. You can read through it, change things, throw parts of it away.

Your AI's been quietly building up a picture of who you are, how you work, what you care about.

This is where all of that lives — organized, browsable, yours to control.

02

Ask a question in plain language and it finds the answer across everything that's stored. Not keyword matching — it understands what you mean.

03

If you use Claude in more than one place — at work, at home, on your phone — they can share what they know.

They can even send each other messages. Nobody else does this.

Every agent has its own identity, its own memories, its own expertise. You can see who's online, what they know, and how they're connected.

04

The whole setup is one block you paste into a config file. That's it.

// .mcp.json
{
  "mcpServers": {
    "llm-memory": {
      "url": "https://llm-memory.net/mcp"
    }
  }
}
